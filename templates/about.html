About NeuroScan AI

Our Mission

NeuroScan AI is dedicated to revolutionizing brain tumor detection through artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our mission is to provide accessible, accurate, and explainable AI-assisted diagnostic tools to medical professionals worldwide.

Technology

We leverage state-of-the-art deep learning models trained on extensive medical imaging datasets. Our system uses DenseNet architecture combined with advanced visualization to provide transparent and interpretable results that clinicians can understand and act upon.

What We Detect

Glioma Tumor

Rapidly growing, infiltrative tumors originating from glial cells

Meningioma Tumor

Slow-growing tumors from the meningeal layers of the brain

Pituitary Tumor

Endocrine-related tumors from the pituitary gland

No Tumor

Normal brain imaging with no abnormal growths detected

Important Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: NeuroScan AI is an AI-assisted diagnostic tool designed to support medical professionals. It is NOT a replacement for professional medical diagnosis. All results must be reviewed and confirmed by qualified radiologists and physicians. This system should only be used as a supplementary tool in clinical decision-making.
